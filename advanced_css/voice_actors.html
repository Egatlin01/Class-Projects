Kenjiro Tsuda

Kenjiro Tsuda is a renowned Japanese voice actor known for his deep, versatile voice and dynamic range. He has brought to life a variety of memorable characters across anime, video games, and drama CDs. Some of his most notable roles include Nanami in "Jujutsu Kaisen", Seto Kaiba in "Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters", and Kishibe in "Chainsaw Man". Tsuda's ability to convey complex emotions and his distinctive vocal tone have made him a favorite among fans and industry professionals alike.

Nobunaga Shimazaki

Nobunaga Shimazaki is a talented Japanese voice actor known for his expressive voice and ability to portray a wide range of characters. He has voiced several prominent roles in anime, including Megumi Fushiguro in "Jujutsu Kaisen", Haruka Nanase in "Free!", and Yukine in "Noragami". Shimazaki's performances are characterized by their emotional depth and authenticity, making him a standout figure in the voice acting community.

Yuichi Nakamura

Yuichi Nakamura is a prominent Japanese voice actor known for his smooth and versatile vocal style. He has portrayed a variety of characters in anime, video games, and drama CDs. Some of his most notable roles include Satoru Gojo in "Jujutsu Kaisen", Gray Fullbuster in "Fairy Tail", and Tatsuya Shiba in "The Irregular at Magic High School". Nakamura's ability to adapt his voice to different character types has earned him widespread acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.
