Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man follows Denji, a teenager crushed by debt and living on the edge until he merges with his devil‑dog companion, Pochita, gaining the terrifying ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws. Thrown into the world of Public Safety Devil Hunters, Denji faces devils born from human fears while navigating a life that’s finally offering him freedom, desire, and heartbreak in equal measure. The series blends brutal action with dark humor and emotional depth, creating a chaotic but strangely human story

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer tells the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a compassionate boy whose peaceful life is shattered when demons slaughter his family and turn his sister Nezuko into one of them. Determined to save her and avenge his loved ones, he joins the Demon Slayer Corps, mastering breathing techniques and facing powerful demons across beautifully animated battles. The series stands out for its emotional storytelling, stunning visuals, and themes of resilience, grief, and hope

Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a cheerful and determined boy who sets out to become a licensed Hunter — an elite adventurer — in hopes of finding his father, who left him behind to pursue the same path. Along the way, Gon forms deep friendships and encounters a world filled with dangerous creatures, complex power systems, and morally challenging foes. The series is known for its strategic battles, layered storytelling, and the way it subverts typical shonen expectations.
